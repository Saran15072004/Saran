The Last Cigarette

Smoking is injurious to health

Yes, smoking is injurious to health and can cause many diseases and harm nearly every organ of the body.
Smoking is the leading cause of preventable death in the United States,
causing more than 480,000 deaths each year

His name is : "ATLEE"

Atlee was a young, ambitious engineering student with a passion for electronics and
a bright future ahead. Despite his potential, he had a habit of smoking every day, a vice he picked up in high school to fit in with friends. As time went on, smoking became a daily routine, despite warnings from loved ones. Atlee excelled in his studies, leading innovative projects and impressing his professors.
However, his health began to deteriorate. A routine check-up revealed the devastating news: Atlee had developed lung cancer. The aggressive cancer progressed quickly. Despite undergoing treatment, Atlee's condition worsened.
He continued working on his projects, but his once bright future was overshadowed by his illness. Surrounded by family and friends, Atlee reflected on his choices with regret. One day, Atlee passed away, a victim of the habit he couldn't overcome.
His story served as a poignant reminder to his loved ones of the importance of health and the dangers of smoking.
